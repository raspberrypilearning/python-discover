In a web browser go to: rpf.io/py-coords
Click ▶ Run. Move the cursor to position the horn then click when you are happy the tip of the mouse pointer ads_click (or your finger on smartphone ) is in the centre.

tips_and_updates Tip: Use the grid to help you. The top left corner of the screen is at coordinates x=0, y=0. The center of the screen is at coordinates x=240, y=200.

Change the unicorn_x and unicorn_y code to make the unicorn appear in random positions each run:
  • unicorn_x = randint(100, 400)
  • unicorn_y = randint(100, 300)

Click ▶ Run and play the game. Play it a few times.


tips_and_updates Tip The coordinates are printed in the output area. Can you pin the horn on the unicorn?

Find else: and grid() lines and comment them out by adding a # at the beginning:

#else:
# grid()

tips_and_updates Tip Lines beginning with # are comments, they are ignored by Python.
